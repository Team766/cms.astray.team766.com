Sponsors

Menlo-Atherton High School

Menlo-Atherton High School is a public high school located in Atherton, California, a small town located on the Peninsula of the Bay Area. Atherton is roughly equidistant from San Francisco and San Jose, and is near Palo Alto, home to Stanford University. Many of the students on our team attend M-A, and our team is also based there. The school supports us by graciously allowing us a room to work in, and access to the Wood Shop, along with monetary donations.

Google

Google is a large corporation based in Mountain View known for running the largest search engine in the world. Founded in 1998 by Sergey Brin and Larry Page in a garage in Menlo Park, California, Google has since become one of the largest companies in the world. Besides the eponymous search engine, Google is known for creating the Android phone operating system and Gmail.

Abbott Fund

Abbott Fund is a charitable organization which helps the world by funding health care projects and charitable endeavors. It supports everybody from doctors in developing countries to robotics teams like ours to fulfill its mission. Abbott Fund is the philanthropic arm of Abbott Laboratories, a large pharmaceutical company based in Illinois which makes drugs, medical appliances and other products that save lives every day.

Sequoia Union High School District

Sequoia Union High School District is the school district, located in San Mateo county, that our high school, M-A, is a part of. The district encompasses 5 public high schools, and 2 charter schools. The other public high schools are Carlmont, Sequoia, and Woodside. Everest and Summit are 2 charter schools which are also part of the district. The Wildhats, FRC Team 100, takes in students from the other high schools in the district.

Intuitive Surgical

Intuitive Surgical is a medical devices corporation best known for creating the Da Vinci line of surgical robots, which are revolutionizing non-invasive surgeries around the world. Intuitive is a big supporter of local robotics teams, supporting several in the Bay Area.

SRI International

SRI International is non-profit research institute located in Menlo Park, across from Menlo-Atherton High School. SRI was originally founded as Stanford Research Institute by Stanford University during the 1920s. Most notably SRI helped create ARPAnet, the predecessor to the Internet, in 1969. SRI later became independent, and now does Research and Development for a wide range of companies and government contractors.

Lockheed Martin

Lockheed Martin is a large defense contractor based out of Bethesda, Maryland. Lockheed Martin was created in 1995 as a result of the merger of the aerospace companies Lockheed Corporation, and Martin Marietta, both of which date back to the early days of heavier than air flight. The modern company is known for its long history of creating advanced military aircraft, along with missiles and space vehicles.
